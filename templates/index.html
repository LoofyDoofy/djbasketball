Introduction

What is Basketball?

Basketball is a team sport which two teams, most commonly of five players each, opposing one another on a court, competing with the objective of shooting a basketball through the defender's hoop while preventing the opposing team from shooting through their own hoop. There are a many skills required to be good in the game, such as dribbling, passing, and shooting, which makes the game unique and exciting to watch.
